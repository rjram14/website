Rotavator plus

Description


The Rotavator Plus is a medium and hard soil rotary tiller with combined features of its predecessors Light and Smart. Available in multiple drives, speeds and blades options; it can produce superior seedbeds for both dry and wet cultivation.


Features

● Multi Speed Gear Box
● Adjustable lower hitch brackets
● L type Blades
● Chain/Gear Driven Side Transmission in oil bath
● CE Guards for European Markets
● Adjustable skids
● Rear Board Springs
